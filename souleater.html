Devour Your Way Through Eternity

You play as a monstrous being with an insatiable hunger for souls. Race through a hauntingly beautiful yet perilous world, devouring everything in your path to fuel your insatiable hunger and stay alive.The core gameplay revolves around classic endless runner mechanics.

Devouring for Survival

Scattered throughout the environment are wispy souls. These come in various forms, from ethereal wisps to tormented humanoids. Collecting them replenishes your soul meter, which acts as your health bar.

Hauntingly Beautiful World

The game world is a 2D landscape bathed in an eerie yet captivating atmosphere. Expect to encounter gothic architecture, desolate graveyards, and crumbling ruins as you race through the endless path. Between runs, you can use collected souls to upgrade your permanent abilities, such as increasing your base movement speed or soul collection range.
